FAQ

Why is the afternoon window so important?

Most people hit their lowest energy point between 3:00 PM and 4:30 PM. This is when sitting turns into dead weight and inertia builds. Tiny movement right now matters more than waiting until later.

Do I have to exercise hard?

No. The goal is not to train hard. The goal is to break the dead zone before it builds. Even 20 seconds of standing counts.

How is my data stored?

All data is stored only on your device using browser local storage. No login required, no cloud sync, no tracking, no nonsense.

Can I get browser notifications?

Yes. You can enable notifications to get reminded when a task is available. Toggling is available in the app settings.

What if I skip a task?

That's fine. If you skip twice in a row, the app shifts to "low energy mode" and gives you the smallest possible tasks to keep momentum alive.
